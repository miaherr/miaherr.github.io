About Me

Hi I'm Mia. I am a Seattle-based photographer, cinematographer, director and editor. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Cinema and Photography from Ithaca College in 2023. I am passionate about archival work and design. My work often explores themes of self-reflection, nostalgia, relationships, and time.
